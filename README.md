## 📖 Overview

Interverse AI is a full-stack AI-powered mock interview platform designed to simulate real interview experiences. Users can generate personalized interview questions based on their job role, technical skills, and experience level, answer them through an interactive interface, and receive AI-generated feedback to improve their interview performance.

The goal of this project is to make interview preparation more practical, personalized, and accessible for students and job seekers.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI-generated interview questions
- 🎯 Personalized interviews based on job role, tech stack, and experience
- 🎤 Webcam and microphone support
- 📊 AI-powered interview feedback
- 🔐 Secure user authentication
- 📂 Interview history dashboard

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Sign in securely using Clerk Authentication.
2. Enter your desired job role, technical skills, and years of experience.
3. AI generates personalized interview questions.
4. Complete the interview using your webcam and microphone.
5. Receive AI-generated feedback based on your responses.
6. Review previous interview sessions anytime from the dashboard.
